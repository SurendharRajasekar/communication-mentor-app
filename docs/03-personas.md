**Depends on:** `01-product-vision.md`, `02-prd.md`

## Scope note
One primary persona for v1, deliberately — with a solo 3-month build, more personas dilute design focus without adding real value at this stage. Additional personas can be introduced post-MVP once the core loop is validated.

## Primary Persona: Priya Sharma, 28 — Software Engineer, Bengaluru

| | |
|---|---|
| **Role** | Mid-level engineer at a mid-size tech company, applying to global/remote roles and increasingly in client-facing calls with US/EU stakeholders |
| **English proficiency** | Strong written English, learned formally in school. Reads and writes confidently — speaking under real-time pressure is where it breaks down |
| **Trigger moment** | An upcoming interview at a global company, or a client call she's been dreading for a week |
| **Pain point** | She knows the answer. She rehearses it in her head. Then in the moment she either freezes, rambles, or over-explains, and replays what she should have said afterward |
| **What she's tried** | General English apps (Duolingo) that don't address real interview/meeting scenarios; YouTube "interview tips" videos with no personalized feedback; practicing out loud alone or with colleagues/family feels awkward |
| **What she wants** | Private, judgment-free practice, and actual evidence she's improving session over session — not just a vague "feel-good" sense of progress |
| **Device habits** | Android phone, practices during her commute or before bed — short pockets of time, not long study sessions |
| **In her words** | "I know the answer. I just don't trust myself to say it clearly when it counts." |

## Explicitly not designing for (v1)
- Senior executives coaching for board-level communication
- Native English speakers wanting general confidence-building
- Casual hobbyist public speakers

(Mirrors the non-goals already locked in `02-prd.md`.)
